Dear

This is an insignificant human being. And this letter that I am writing is one of hope.

Scroll Down

I have often looked at the night sky and wondered about the millions of stars and the planets around them.

There is so much of possibility that we are surrounded by many others like us, scattered all through out this universe.

Yet, a query remains in my mind, that we might very well be alone. And if that is true then, I look towards you for some hope.

In this universe of infinite possibilities, everything starting from a bright star till a small pebble are moving towards chaos, and destruction.

And the only thing amidst them that I find in order, is life.

Calming like a dove

Soothing like a giant tree

And beautiful like your smile.

And if we are alone, the responsibility falls on us to fight against this chaos.

To preserve this order, for the beauty within you wields such power.

You belong to a race which conquered the oceans, defeated death and stepped on the moon.

So please look firmly into my eyes and give me hope that you stand strong, against everything that may come.

In Wait

Yours, Niladri.

Why I Made This ?

And it was a difficult time for her. I felt very helpless as I couldnot connect with her. This on my part is just an attempt to listen to anyone who wants to talk.
